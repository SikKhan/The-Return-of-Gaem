Little privileged Riding Hood

Author: Faisal

Once upon a time there was a foreign student who lived in a complex near the University with her mother. One day her grandmother gave her a very nice scholarship. That is why everybody in the complex called her Little privileged Riding Hood. One day…

Privileged: You called me mother?

Mother: Yes, Little privileged Riding Hood. Your grandmother is very sick. I want you to go to her house and take her this little basket with some hot water and fruits.

Privileged: Yes Mother.

Mother: But don´t stop in Sanlitun, and don´t talk to strangers. Do as I say - go straight to your grandmother’s house.

Little privileged Riding Hood ignored her Mother's request and went to Sanlitun, with the little basket for her grandmother. Along the way, she saw many beautiful clubs.

Privileged: (Buys alcohol) What beautiful alcohol! I will take some to my grandmother.

She left the little basket on the ground to pick up some more alcohol, when suddenly…

Stranger: Good morning. Little privileged Riding Hood!

Privileged:(smiling) Good morning, Mr. Stranger.

Stranger: Where are you going so early?

Privileged: I am going to my grandmother´s house to take her this little basket with some Hot water and fruits (and alcohol).

Stranger: Does your grandmother live far from here, Little privileged Riding Hood?

Privileged: No, Mr. Stranger, she lives just across Sanlitun in the first little house in the complex - the one with the white door.

Stranger: Good-bye, Little privileged Riding Hood. I hope she gets better. And look, do you see that road?

Privileged: Yes, I do.

Stranger: (pointing) Just follow that road and you will get to your grandmother´s house.

Little Privileged Riding Hood kept looking for alcohol disobeying her mother who told her not to stop in Sanlitun and to get to her grandmother´s house. Meanwhile, the Stranger knocked at Little Privileged Riding Hood grandmother´s house.

(Knock) (knock) (knock)

Grandmother: Who's there

Professor: (soft voice) It´s me, grandmother, Little Privileged Riding Hood, and I brought you a basket with alcohol.

Grandmother: Come in my little angel, push the door.

As soon as the Stranger entered, he hid Little Privileged Riding Hood´s grandmother. Then he put on her nightgown, jumped into bed and waited for the student. A few minutes later Little Privileged Riding Hood arrived at her grandmother´s house. She knocked at the door.

Privileged: Grandmother, it´s me, your granddaughter, Little Privileged Riding Hood. I came to bring you a little basket with some hot water and fruits (whispers) and some alcohol.

Professor: Come in my little angel, push the door.

Narrator: Little Privileged Riding Hood entered the little house. She went straight to her grandmother´s room, and in the bed there was the Professor dressed with her grandmother´s clothes.

Professor: Come in, my little angel. Rest for a while.

Privileged: (looking puzzled) Grandmother…your voice sounds so ew. Is something the matter?

Professor: I just have a bit of a cold.

Privileged: Oh, what big ears you have, grandmother!

Professor: it’s so I can hear who's eating in the class

Privileged: And what big eyes you have!

Professor: So I can see who's cheating

Privileged: Oh, grandmother, and what big teeth you have.

Professor:(jumping out of bed) All the better to laugh at you with! Ha, ha, ha, ha, ha!

You have failed ALL of your classes

Mother: Ha, ha, ha, ha, ha! You will now be forced to work in the family business forever!

The End!

The Animal Rights Activist

Writer: Faisal

There once was a programmer, said programmer encountered a bug in his AI code, the programmer was confused at first, assumed it was merely a minor typo however after thirty minutes of checking his code he found no such typo.

He searched on the internet but came out empty handed, he asked his colleagues but to no avail, but then a senior developer said this "Go for a walk, there you will find your answers"

The programmer thought the senior developer was merely trying to cover his lack of experience but he begrudgingly agreed. After walking for what seemed like an eternity the young programmer saw an ant hill

He mustered every ounce of frustration and kicked the ant hill, causing a massive cloud of sand and ants, afterward a massive army of ants erupted out of the remnantants of the ant hill.

The young programmer noticed that each group of ants had their own task, some were hunting, others were repairing, it was then that the young programmer finally figured out how to deal with that fatal error and went on to become an animal rights activist.
